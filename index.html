Excisus
Modular Typography

Excisus is a modular typeface based on existing typography present in the city of Coimbra and it was designed soly for use in Headers and Titles therefore it is not easy to read if used in body text. It’s name is the latin translation of the word “sculped”. It is so because of the process we took in order to design it. Excisus is made out of 4 diferent modules, all retrieved from different typical typgraphy found throughout Coimbra. These modules are assembled together creating an unique typeface that bridges the old with the contemporary.
